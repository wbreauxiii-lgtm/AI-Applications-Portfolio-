# Willie Breaux | AI Applications Portfolio

This repository is a public version of my applied AI work developed through college coursework and refined for a professional audience.

The point of this portfolio is to show how I think about AI when the stakes are real: where it helps, where it breaks, what it costs to get wrong, and what responsible deployment actually requires.

## Who I Am

I am Willie Breaux. My interest in AI is practical before it is fashionable. I care about systems that have to work under pressure, produce defensible outcomes, and hold up when questions of bias, fraud, trust, safety, and accountability stop being theoretical.

This portfolio tracks that line of thinking across finance, manufacturing, government, law enforcement, healthcare, agriculture, blockchain, retail, smart cities, and media.
