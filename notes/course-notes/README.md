# Course Notes

These notes are the broader record behind the case studies.

They cover the course topics that shaped how I think about AI in practice: not just what the tools can do, but where they fail, where they distort decisions, and where they need tighter oversight.

## Topics Covered

- AI philosophy and ethics
- AI in healthcare and agriculture
- bias and health inequity
- AI in retail
- AI in finance, smart cities, and media
- AI and blockchain
- AI in finance

Each file in this folder is a public-safe version of the original coursework. Student identifiers were redacted for publication.
